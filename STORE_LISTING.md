## Full Description

### App Store / Play Store

**Your operating system for deep work.**

FlowOS isn't another to-do list. It's a complete system for intentional productivity — built around the loop of *intention → focused work → recovery → reflection*.

**🧠 Energy-Aware Task Management**
Every task has an energy level — Deep, Medium, or Light. FlowOS helps you match your tasks to your current energy, so you're always working on the right thing at the right time.

**⏱️ Focus Sessions with XP**
Start focus sessions (Pomodoro or 90-min Deep Work mode) and earn XP for every minute of focused work. Track quality, not just time.

**🌱 Grow Your Flow Garden**
Every focused session plants a seed in your Flow Garden. Watch flowers bloom and trees grow as you complete your goals, turning your focus into a living visual representation of your progress.

**🛡️ App Shielding & Focus Protection**
Block distracting apps (like Instagram, TikTok, or YouTube) on Android during active focus sessions. Choose between Gentle (helpful prompts) or Firm (strict accessibility shields) to protect your attention block.

**🌙 Sleep Mode Blocker**
Schedule bedtime hours to block distracting apps automatically. Set custom rules for weekdays and weekends to protect your sleep schedule and build better wind-down routines.

**🔥 Streaks & Achievements**
Build daily streaks, unlock achievements, and level up. FlowOS gamifies the boring parts of productivity without turning it into guiltware.

**📊 Daily Reports & AI Insights**
Every day ends with an honest report — your Flow Score (A+ to F), AI-generated insights, attention costs, and a tip for tomorrow. No toxic positivity, just real feedback.

**📱 Attention Cost Tracking**
Self-report your scrolling time and see exactly how it affects your daily score. Recovery actions (walk, breathe, stretch) earn XP back.

**🪞 Weekly Review**
A guided 5-minute flow every Sunday. AI-generated wins, growth areas, and reflection questions based on your actual data.

**🧠 Brain Dump → AI Sort**
Dump everything on your mind. AI sorts it into actionable tasks with energy levels, friction scores, and suggested order.

**🌲 Ambient Sounds**
Focus with binaural beats, rain, café ambiance, or forest sounds. Gapless looping with fade-out on session end.

**🎨 Unlockable Themes**
Earn new themes as you level up: Deep Space, Dark Forest, Midnight Ice, Warm Sunrise.

**☁️ Sync Across Devices**
Sign in with Apple or Google. Your data syncs across iOS and Android. Works fully offline.

**🔒 Privacy First**
Local-first storage. No ads. On-device usage analysis stays on your phone — never sold or shared. AI insights processed securely through our backend — your data never goes directly to third parties.
