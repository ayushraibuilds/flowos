# FlowOS Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Overview

FlowOS ("the App") is a productivity and focus tracking application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address and authentication credentials (if you create an account)
- **Task Data:** Task titles, descriptions, energy levels, completion status
- **Focus Sessions:** Session type, duration, quality grades
- **Energy Check-ins:** Energy level readings (1-5 scale) with optional notes
- **Brain Dump Text:** Free-text entries you submit for AI sorting

### Data Generated by the App
- **XP & Achievements:** Experience points earned and achievements unlocked
- **Daily/Weekly Reports:** Aggregated productivity scores and AI-generated insights
- **Scroll Tracking:** On Android, we access local app usage stats (only if permission is granted). For other platforms, this is self-reported.
- **Browsing Data (Chrome Extension):** Website URLs, categories, and visit durations (only if you install the Chrome extension)

### Local Device Telemetry & Permissions (Android)
To power the app blocking and daily scoring features, FlowOS integrates with local Android system APIs. This data is kept strictly private, processed on-device, and never sold or shared:
- **Usage Access (UsageStatsManager):** Queries local app usage minutes (e.g. social media apps) to calculate distraction scores and stay within set scroll budgets.
- **Accessibility Service:** Observes app launch events during active focus sessions to block or redirect distracting applications.
- **Notification Access:** Optionally used to log ambient interruptions (notifications received) for focus metrics.
*These integrations are entirely local and optional. FlowOS works in a manual-logging mode if permissions are not granted.*

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- **Contacts, Photos, or Files:** We do not access any personal media
- **Location Data:** We do not track your location
- **Advertising Identifiers:** We do not collect advertising IDs

## How We Use Your Data

1. **Core Functionality:** Your tasks, sessions, and XP are stored to provide the app's features
2. **AI Insights:** Task and session data is sent to our backend server, which uses Google's Gemini API to generate personalized daily reports, break suggestions, and task sorting. We send aggregated metrics, not raw personal content
3. **Cloud Sync:** If you create an account, your data is synced to Supabase (our cloud provider) so you can access it across devices
4. **No Selling:** We never sell your data to third parties
5. **No Advertising:** We do not serve ads or share data with ad networks

## Data Storage & Security

- **Local First:** All data is stored locally on your device first using an encrypted SQLite database
- **Cloud Sync (Optional):** If you sign in, data is synced to Supabase with Row-Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
- **Encryption:** All data in transit uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **AI Processing:** AI requests are processed through our backend proxy. We do not send your data directly to third-party AI providers from your device

## Your Rights

- **Offline Mode:** You can use FlowOS entirely offline without creating an account
- **Data Export:** You can request a full export of your data at any time
- **Data Deletion:** You can delete all your data from Settings → Account → Delete All Data. This removes data from both your device and our servers
- **Account Deletion:** Contact us at privacy@flowos.app to delete your account entirely

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Authentication & cloud sync | Encrypted user data |
| Google Gemini API | AI-generated insights | Aggregated metrics (via backend proxy) |
| Google/Apple Sign-In | Authentication | OAuth tokens only |

## Children's Privacy

FlowOS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
